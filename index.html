About Me

I’m a passionate and detail-oriented IT professional with a strong foundation in modern web technologies, graphic design, and blockchain innovation. I specialize in creating responsive, user-centered digital solutions that combine functionality with aesthetic appeal.

My expertise spans front-end and back-end development, including HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript (ES6+), and frameworks such as Bootstrap, React, and Node.js. I’m a lifelong learner who thrives in dynamic, collaborative environments and constantly seeks to merge creativity, technology, and problem-solving to deliver impactful digital experiences.
